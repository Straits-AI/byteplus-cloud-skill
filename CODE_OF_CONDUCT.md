# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while addressing harmful impact directly.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on evidence and user safety.
- Respect privacy and never pressure contributors to disclose cloud credentials or
  customer information.
- Accept maintainer decisions about scope, security, and moderation gracefully.

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, doxxing, sexualized attention,
trolling, and publishing another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when behavior violates this policy.

For a confidential report, use this repository's private vulnerability reporting
channel and prefix the title with `Community conduct:`. Do not include cloud
credentials or unrelated sensitive data. Reports will be reviewed by repository
maintainers and handled as privately as practical.
